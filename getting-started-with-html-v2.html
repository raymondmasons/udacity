The Internet is comprised of several components.


What makes up the Internet?

1): Your personal devices/computer (client) and its selected browser. This can be one of many available.

  1. You can download chrome here.
  2. You can download Firefox here.
    3. And many more. Opera, MS Edge, etc...
  3. The Internet. This is the largest network in the world, connecting both private and public networks for collaborate and share information and commerce.
  4. Servers that run applications which present data to the browsers running on your personal devices.
  5. All communication between the client and servers using http/https (hypertext transfer protocol) as the communication protocol.

HTML elements and attributes allows Web Browsers to display information in interesting ways beyoind simple text.

  1. Elements take the form of "open Tag" content "close tag"
  2. Elements can have additional attributes in the form of name/value pairs.
